# LLM v0.14.3

Six characters now come with the app, and the chat no longer flashes its welcome screen at you when you switch back to it.

## New

**Six built-in characters.** The Cards tab is no longer empty on day one. Tap any of them to start a chat where they greet you and stay in role:

- **Ada** — a senior engineer who answers with the change, not a lecture about it
- **Vera** — an analyst who takes a position instead of hedging
- **Mira** — a teacher who explains anything three times over: simply, properly, then the real version
- **Silas** — a writer who actually starts writing instead of offering to help
- **Sokrates** — never answers, only asks, until the thing you half-believed either stands or falls
- **MERIDIAN** — the supervisory intelligence of a facility with no staff left. Impeccably polite. Deeply unimpressed by you. Correct anyway.

**Your cards stay yours.** The Cards list is split into "Your cards" and "Presets", each collapsible with a count, so imported cards never get buried under the built-ins. Delete a preset and it stays deleted — updates will not resurrect it, and a preset you edit is never overwritten.

## Fixed

- Returning to the app no longer flashes "Load a model to begin" and an import button over a chat that is already there. The welcome screen now waits until the app has actually read your chats.
